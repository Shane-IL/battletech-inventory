Two big corrections that nearly cancelled. Centurion CN9-YLW is 957, not ~1,350 — the canonical Yen-Lo-Wang is a stripped 50-tonner (AC/20 + titanium nails, no LRM), so it's a glass cannon that brings the AC/20 but dies fast in an assault lance. Banshee BNC-3S is 1,751, not ~1,290 — the 3026 Defiance rebuild (2× PPC + AC/10 + 4 ML + SRM-6) is far beefier than the old undergunned 3E. Net only +68 vs the estimate; the lance is legal at 5,940 with ~60 of headroom. If you'd rather a tougher second AC/20, the CN9-YLW2 (Gauss + hatchet + TSM) is 1,415 — but that pushes the lance to 6,398, over cap, so you'd drop the Thunderbolt to fit it.

Fielding. The three IS lances share no minis — run any or all together. The Clan Stars overlap heavily on the Adder, Nova, Timber Wolf and Gargoyle, so treat them as alternative forces: one Star per game. Configs are listed exactly so the record-sheet pull is unambiguous (e.g. Nova S, Adder A, Timber Wolf B) — the cheaper alt-configs are where the value lives, per the Clan Variants page.

Pilot skill is the cleanest dial to hit exactly 6,000: a Regular→Veteran bump adds ~32% of that 'Mech's BV. Set it in MegaMek and let it compute the exact total. All BVs above are MUL BV 2.0 at 4/5 (Regular).

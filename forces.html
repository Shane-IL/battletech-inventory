Two big corrections that nearly cancelled. Centurion CN9-YLW is 957, not ~1,350 — the canonical Yen-Lo-Wang is a stripped 50-tonner (AC/20 + titanium nails, no LRM), so it's a glass cannon that brings the AC/20 but dies fast in an assault lance. If you'd rather a tougher second AC/20, the CN9-YLW2 (Gauss + hatchet + TSM) is 1,415 — but that pushes the lance to 6,398, over cap, so you'd drop the Thunderbolt to fit it.

Fielding. The six IS lances (Long Reach, Hammerfall, Mixed Spear, Quicksilver, Sledgehammer, Kurita Ranger) share no minis — run any or all together. The ComStar Level II and its Battle Demi field the same six new ComStar minis, so those two are alternatives to each other, but they don't overlap the IS lances. The Clan Stars overlap heavily on the Adder, Nova, Timber Wolf and Gargoyle, so treat them as alternative forces: one Star per game. Configs are listed exactly so the record-sheet pull is unambiguous (e.g. Nova S, Adder A, Timber Wolf B) — the cheaper alt-configs are where the value lives, per the Clan Variants page.

Pilot skill is the cleanest dial to hit exactly 6,000: a Regular→Veteran bump adds ~32% of that 'Mech's BV. Set it in MegaMek and let it compute the exact total. All BVs above are MUL BV 2.0 at 4/5 (Regular).

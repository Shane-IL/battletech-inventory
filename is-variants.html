How this was built. Every BV 2.0 figure is pulled straight from the Master Unit List (masterunitlist.info), the official catalogue. For each chassis I show the variant you own (★) plus a handful of buildable Introductory / Standard alternatives introduced by ~3067, sorted by BV so the cheapest option is obvious. A few chassis (Loki Mk II, Rokurokubi, Shiro, Regent) are Dark Age designs whose only catalogued configs are post-3062 — those are shown and flagged post-3062.

Weapon loadouts aren’t listed — the MUL bulk endpoint exposes BV, era, rules level and role but not the classic weapons line, so rather than hand-type (and risk fudging) ~280 loadouts, each row shows intro-year · rules level. Pull any sheet in MegaMek for the full armament.

All figures are BV 2.0 at Regular (4/5); multiply by ~1.32 for Veteran (3/4). 49 of 54 owned variants matched the MUL cleanly.

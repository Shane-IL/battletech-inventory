The UrbanMech LAM (UM-R60L) you own isn’t in the canon list or the MUL — it’s an apocryphal Land-Air ’Mech (the meme made real), so it has no canon BV and needs Land-Air ’Mech rules to field.

How this was built. Variants are restricted to canon designs (designer = Catalyst Game Labs, per mordel.net), and every BV 2.0 figure is cross-checked and aligned to that canon list. For each chassis I show the variant you own (★) plus a handful of buildable Introductory / Standard alternatives introduced by ~3067, sorted by BV so the cheapest option is obvious. A few chassis (Loki Mk II, Rokurokubi, Shiro, Regent) are Dark Age designs whose only catalogued configs are post-3062 — those are shown and flagged post-3062.

Weapon loadouts are pulled from MegaMek’s mm-data unit files (the canonical .mtf records), aggregated per variant — including physical weapons (e.g. the Yen-Lo-Wang’s Hatchet); each row also notes intro-year · rules level. Loadouts are the stock record-sheet armament — pull any sheet in MegaMek for crit placement and ammo.

All figures are BV 2.0 at Regular (4/5); multiply by ~1.32 for Veteran (3/4). 60 of 60 owned variants matched the catalogue cleanly.

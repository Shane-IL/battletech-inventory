Lance Builder

Rolls a force from your owned minis under a BV cap. 79 IS · 33 Clan minis on hand.

Clan Exclude experimental Painted only

How it works. Default is a 4-’Mech Inner Sphere lance; toggle Clan for a Star (defaults to 5), and pick Force size — arena duel (1), reduced (3), lance (4) or star (5). Exclude experimental (on by default) drops the Proliferation Cycle primitives. The builder draws only from ’Mechs you own, filters by faction / size / paint / intro-year, then runs a randomized search (GRASP: ~250 diverse constructions plus local swap refinement) over whole candidate forces — not one blended score per ’Mech. Each complete force is judged on BV efficiency, short/medium/long-range damage coverage (stacking one range band has diminishing returns, so a good force needs teeth at more than one range), total durability, and how the force's overall movement profile fits the archetype — so a ’Mech's value depends on what else is already in the lance, not just its own stats. Hit Build to reroll; ties within 4% of the best-scoring force are picked at random for variety. Set Gunnery/Piloting (default 4/5) and BVs are recomputed with the standard TechManual skill multiplier before fitting the cap — e.g. 3/4 = ×1.32 (base BV shown in parens when skill ≠ 4/5). Skill mix presets (all-Veteran, one Elite, one Elite + two Vets, …) put your best pilots in your most valuable ’Mechs for the selected archetype and fit the mixed-skill total under the cap; each row shows its skill, Alpha-Strike damage (S/M/L), and movement (ground/jump). Archetype reshapes what the search rewards — Gunline favors long-range firepower and a slow, dug-in force; Brawler favors short/medium punch and a deliberate mix of fast flankers with a tough anchor; Fast strike rewards uniform high speed across the whole lance; Assault wall favors durability and staying put. Canon BV 2.0 values. A few Clan-tech 'Mechs that came in IS packs (Curtain's Regent, the GDL Gargoyle, a Dragoons Timber Wolf) are tagged IS/Clan; they're kept in Clan rolls only — left out of the IS pool to avoid skewing it, so build those IS-with-a-Clan-'Mech lances by hand.

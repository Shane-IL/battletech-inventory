Lance Builder

Rolls a force from your owned minis under a BV cap. 80 IS · 32 Clan minis on hand.

Clan Exclude experimental Painted only

How it works. Default is a 4-’Mech Inner Sphere lance; toggle Clan for a Star (defaults to 5), and pick Force size — arena duel (1), reduced (3), lance (4) or star (5). Exclude experimental (on by default) drops the Proliferation Cycle primitives. The builder draws only from ’Mechs you own, filters by faction / size / paint / intro-year, then fills toward the BV cap (200 random draws, keeps the fullest legal force). Hit Build to reroll. Set Gunnery/Piloting (default 4/5) and BVs are recomputed with the standard TechManual skill multiplier before fitting the cap — e.g. 3/4 = ×1.32 (base BV shown in parens when skill ≠ 4/5). Skill mix presets (all-Veteran, one Elite, one Elite + two Vets, …) assign the better pilots to your highest-BV ’Mechs and fit the mixed-skill total under the cap; each row shows its skill. Canon BV 2.0 values. A few Clan-tech 'Mechs that came in IS packs (Curtain's Regent, the GDL Gargoyle, a Dragoons Timber Wolf) are tagged IS/Clan and can be drawn into either force. Paint status is read live from your inventory page (same browser).
